About N8iV Intelligence

We Built the Attribution Layer
Most Agencies Won't Touch.

We're analysts, not marketers. We built N8iV Intelligence to give businesses the closed-loop data infrastructure that was previously only available to enterprise companies with 7-figure analytics budgets.

Our Philosophy

Revenue is the only metric that matters.

Most marketing agencies are optimized for activity, not outcomes. They report on clicks, impressions, and form fills because those numbers are easy to produce, easy to inflate, and easy to show in a polished deck. The actual question — how many dollars of revenue did this campaign generate? — is rarely answered honestly.

N8iV Intelligence was built on a different premise. We start with closed revenue and work backwards. Every campaign, channel, and creative is evaluated against a single standard: did it contribute to a customer who paid? Everything else is noise.

Our mission is to make closed-loop attribution a standard practice, not a luxury. The infrastructure exists. The APIs are available. The only thing missing is the analyst who knows how to connect them — and the discipline to stand behind the numbers that come out.

Methodology

Our Attribution Framework.

Four principles guide every engagement. They're not aspirational values — they're operational requirements that every report, audit, and model is built against.

01

Revenue Verification First

Every attribution number is verified against CRM-closed revenue, not platform reports. If we can't trace a number to an actual closed deal, we don't report it.

02

First-Party Data Priority

Server-side signals and CRM events are prioritized over third-party pixels. We build attribution systems that will outlast every cookie policy update.

03

Model Calibration

Attribution models are calibrated specifically to each client's sales cycle length and deal complexity — not pulled from generic platform presets.

04

Monthly Accountability

Signed reports every month. We stand behind every number we put in front of you. If the data changes, we explain why. No hiding behind dashboard complexity.
