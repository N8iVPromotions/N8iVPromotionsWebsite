About

Turning Marketing
Into Certainty.

We're a team of analysts,
not marketers.

N8iV was built on a simple frustration: too many smart businesses were making million-dollar media decisions based on click-through rates and form fills that never tracked back to actual revenue. We fix that. Our job is to close the loop between ad spend and closed deals — with data, not dashboards.

We work with independent businesses and boutique agencies who are tired of paying for marketing they can't measure. Every engagement starts with a full audit of your current tracking architecture, and ends with a model that tells you — in plain language — which channels, campaigns, and creatives are actually driving revenue and which are just burning budget.
