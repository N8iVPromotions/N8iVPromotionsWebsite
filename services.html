Pipeline Source Modeling

Most B2B companies have sales cycles measured in months, not days. Standard attribution models built for e-commerce don't account for this. We build models that do.

Our pipeline source modeling applies six attribution model variants — first-touch, last-touch, linear, time-decay, position-based, and data-driven — across your full historical pipeline. Each model is calibrated to your average sales cycle length and deal complexity, then reconciled against actual closed revenue in your CRM.

The result: a quarterly pipeline report that shows which channels initiate deals, which accelerate them, and which channels are responsible for final conversion — with attribution credit weighted accordingly. Updated every quarter as your business evolves.
