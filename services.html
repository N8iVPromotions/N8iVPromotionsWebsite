Closed-Loop
Attribution

Most attribution tools stop at the click. We go all the way to the close. By stitching your ad platforms, CRM, and call-tracking data into one unified model, we can tell you exactly which campaign, audience, and creative earned each deal — in dollars, not impressions.

Our always-on setup runs silently in the background. Every new deal that enters your CRM is automatically traced back to its origin touchpoints. You get a living view of channel ROI that updates as your pipeline moves.

Channel
Performance Audit

A structured 14-day engagement where we comb through every active paid channel — Meta, Google, LinkedIn, TikTok — and surface exactly where budget is being wasted, mismeasured, or misattributed.

You receive a written findings report with prioritized recommendations: quick wins you can act on this week, structural fixes to schedule, and spend reallocation guidance backed by your own historical data. No fluff, no agency-speak.

Pipeline Source
Modeling

Your sales cycle isn't a single touchpoint — it's a sequence of influences spread across weeks or months. We build multi-touch attribution models that reflect how your deals actually close: first-touch for brand awareness, last-touch for conversion triggers, and assist credit for everything in between.

Delivered quarterly as a re-calibration of your channel weighting, so budget decisions stay grounded in what your pipeline is actually telling you — not last year's assumptions.
